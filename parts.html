What parts are required to build a custom mechanical keyboard?
  • Case
  • PCB
  • Plate
  • Keycaps
  • Switches

Case: The container that holds all the other components

PCB: The brain behind a functional mechanical keyboard

Plate: The structure that holds the switches in place with the PCB

  • Polycarbonate
  • Acrylic
  • POM
  • FR4
  • Aluminium
  • Brass

Keycaps: The contact point with the keyboard

  • ABS
  • PBT
  • POM
  • Wood
  • Resin
  • Aluminium
  • Brass

Switches: The keystroke register

    Top Housing
  • POM
  • Polycarbonate
  • Nylon
  • UHMWPE

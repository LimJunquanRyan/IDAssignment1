Important Terminology to know

Terminologies are common within the custom mechanical keyboard hobby as it it a very small and niche hobby with highly technical terms that are often simplified into simpler phrases and terminology

Common words to describe sound signature:

  • Thocc/Thock: Deep and Rich
  • Clack: Higher-pitched
  • Creamy: Smooth with slight depth and viscosity
  • Poppy: As the name suggests, slight pop
  • Marbley: slight pop and reflective resonance
  • Sharp: unpleasant high-pitched
  • Ping: metal ringing
  • Resonance: slight vibrations
  • Clean: no vibrations
  • Muted: one-dimensional and quiet
  • Full: nice, round variance in pitch

Material Abbreviations

  • ABS: Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene
  • PBT: Poly-Butylene Terephthalate
  • POM: Poly-Oxy-Methylene
  • UHMWPE: Ultra-High-Molecular-Weight-Poly-Ethylene
  • FR4: Flame-Retardant Woven Glass-Reinforced Epoxy Resin AKA Fiberglass AKA PCB Material
